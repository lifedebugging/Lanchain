# Agentic RAG systems

We're here finally to the last of RAG, an **agentic RAG**.

**What is an Agentic RAG**?

Agentic RAG system is where AI agents intelligently decide when and how to search your documents to answer questions. Unlike traditional RAG that always searches regardless of need, agentic RAG gives your AI the autonomy to determine whether retrieval is necessary - answering directly when it has the knowledge.
